The Cube at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD)
October 11 to October 29

What is one thing you want to do before you die? We don’t know when our last day on this planet will be — if you were to die tomorrow, could you say that you truly lived the way you wanted?

One Thing was an interactive installation inspired by the artist's fear of death. Hopes, dreams, and goals were displayed in The Cube, encouraging participants to share their aspirations with the public and do things they have always wanted to do before dying. One Thing was also the first installation in The Cube that incorporated user input. Participants who visited The Cube in person engaged in the installation through writing, but anyone who could not visit in person were welcome to participate through the Submit page of the installation’s website. Users from anywhere in the world could submit their answers, adding an international aspect to this project. Digital answers were brought together in a physical space, providing inspiration to and from people in countries around the world.
